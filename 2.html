Meera's Music

I love indie. My favorite indie band is Death Cab for Cutie. I've been a fan of theirs for as long as I can remember, and I was fortunate enough to be able to see them live in concert recently. It was a very genuine, heartfelt performance and as such, a very positive experience. Fun fact: it is actually impossible for me to choose a favorite album from their discography; there's something I love about each one.
