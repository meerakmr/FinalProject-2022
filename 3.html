Meera's Music

I love metal. I'm not sure what exactly drew me to the genre, but nonetheless, metal has been one of my most-listened-to genres for the past few years. It is a source of comfort for me, and I sometimes use it as an outlet. My two favorite metal bands are Motionless in White and Ice Nine Kills, both of whom I got the chance to see in concert.
